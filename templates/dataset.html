Customer Segment Explanation

Cluster 0 – Average Customers
These customers have moderate income and moderate spending habits. They represent a stable group of regular buyers and are important for maintaining consistent sales.

Cluster 1 – Premium Customers
These customers have high income and high spending behavior. Businesses should target them with premium products, loyalty programs, and exclusive offers.

Cluster 2 – Budget Customers
These customers have lower income and low spending scores. They respond better to discounts, offers, and affordable product options.

Cluster 3 – High Value Customers
These customers have higher income and relatively strong purchasing power. Marketing strategies should focus on personalized services and high-quality products.

Cluster 4 – Low Engagement Customers
These customers have income but low spending behavior. Companies can increase engagement through promotions, advertisements, or loyalty incentives.
